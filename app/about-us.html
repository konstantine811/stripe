Why ixLayer?

Product at ixLayer

We think that building a genetic product is a problem rooted in technology and regulatory, not science. Culture at ixLayer

ixLayer is not just about writing world-class code, we care deeply about design and user engagement. It is not enough just to sell a product or service but to truly create value for the user.
